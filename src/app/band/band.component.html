BAND

Born in the very west of Austria, where the world is nothing more than a cracked fa√ßade of a Shire-like and sheltered existence - Gallowhead and their music try to peek through the cracks and shine a light on the other side of the same dirty coin.

Growling bass tones reverberate angrily and snug tightly around groovy drum rhythms, creating a rigid surface, only to be cut through by a pair of distorted guitars on the brink of complete failure.

The songs are accompanied by the occasional dreamy melody, only to be sucked back into the swamp of nothingness by shouts and growls of unforgiveness.

The lyrics, just like the music, revolve around the irony of life, bodily fluids, pain, and its acceptance.
