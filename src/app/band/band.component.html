Gallowhead’s origin goes back to when the world was midway through the pandemic and
was started as a project by people who love all kinds of heavy music.

Since then they have released three records - either DIY or with some help from their dear
friend at Zanns Records.

They’ve played countless shows in various venues all around Europe in countries such as Austria, Switzerland, Germany, Italy and France.

When the band is asked how they would describe their sound, the answer is usually not an instant one and a bit long-winded, before giving up and saying something along the lines of “music by people who would like to play fast but are only able to play slowly”.

A blend of all the bands they love and influence them meets the uncompromising attitude of working hard and playing whatever riff and melody or drum beat they want.

For fans of: Sludge, Doom, (Post)-Hardcore, Death Metal, Anti Fascist Black Metal
